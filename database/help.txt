Welcome to Benny's Adventure Team!

.create           - create a profile

.p or .profile    - view your profile stats

.daily            - claim your daily rewards. Keep your streak to earn more rewards!

.hunt [number]    - spend your resins to collect moras and primos

.pull [number]    - spend your primos to pull characters and get more bonus stats

.boss             - spend your resins to challenge the boss and rank up in the leaderboard!

.gamba [mora]     - spend your moras to get random bonus stats up to 4 stats. Be careful the bonus can be negative too!

.char             - view your characters and their constellation

.rank             - see where are you in the leaderboard!
